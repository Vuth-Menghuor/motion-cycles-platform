Component Structure
Main Component:

ReviewsPage or ReviewsSection

Child Components:

RatingOverview - The top section with 4.5 rating and rating distribution bars
ReviewsList - The customer feedback section with individual reviews
ReviewForm - The "Submit Your Review" form at the bottom

Smaller Sub-components:

RatingStars - Reusable star rating component
RatingBar - Individual rating distribution bar
ReviewCard - Individual review item

Recommended Tools/Libraries for Charts & Ratings
For Rating Charts & Visualizations:

Recharts - Great for the rating distribution bars
Chart.js - Another solid option for bar charts
D3.js - Most flexible but requires more setup


src
 └── components
      └── reviews
          ├── ReviewsPage.vue          # Main container (or ReviewsSection.vue if nested in a page)
          │
          ├── overview

          │   ├── RatingStars.vue      # Reusable star rating display

          │
          ├── list
          │   ├── ReviewsList.vue      # Displays multiple reviews
          │   └── ReviewCard.vue       # Single review card (author, comment, stars)
          │
          └── form
              └── ReviewForm.vue       # Form for submitting reviews
